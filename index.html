A LITTLE ABOUT WG FITNESS

Personal and group fitness training located in Sydney’s Eastern Suburbs, WG Fitness aims to make health and fitness fun and one of the most important priorities of your week. Wayne can do this by encouraging and challenging you in a way that gets you to ask more of yourself both with the intensity at which you are training and with the food and lifestyle choices you are making. I aim to give you the awareness so as these two ingredients become second nature. The majority of my clients are time poor, so I ensure that their fitness sessions with me are the most productive form of exercise that they do that week.

I believe being happy, which goes hand in hand with being fit and healthy. Sometimes that may require sacrifices in other areas of your life. I don’t believe in diets, 6 week boot camps or anything that is looking for a quick solution. I believe that eating healthy, plenty of rest and quality time productive exercise is a lifestyle!
